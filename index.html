  Delicious Vegan Donuts in Leipzig

After several months of construction work we are happy and proud to announce that we are finally opening. Welcome to the first vegan donut café in Leipzig! Come by, grab one of our homemade donuts and a drink and enjoy the sun outside on our terrace. See you soon :)

  Our Donuts

We offer a variety of vegan donuts such as:

  • Salted Caramel
  • Bee Sting
  • Tiramisu
  • German style "Berliner" (with jam filling)
  • Cinnamon Sugar
  • Rainbow Sprinkles
  • Lemon Cheesecake
  • Chocolate-Cherry

...and monthly changing special donuts.

Don't hesitate to approach us if you have any kind of allergies and we will gladly provide you with information. We also always have two gluten-free donut options on the menu.

For a chance to win a donut box of 6 we have a monthly competition where you can leave us a message via e-mail with a suggestion for a new donut style. Feel free to use the contact form below. We will get in touch with you should yours be selected. Good luck! :)

  Café

We are welcoming you in our newly opened Sticky Donuts Café in Leipzig-Plagwitz, located on Karl-Heine-Straße. We have indoor as well as outdoor seating available and a separate quiet room for those who would like to enjoy their donut in silence or are easily sensorily overwhelmed. Our café is also suitable for remote work: there is free WiFi for you to use.

All our products are plant-based and homemade. We have a variety of drinks: Tea, Coffee, Soft Drinks, homemade Masala Chai and more. You can view our full menu here: (link)

Your pet is very welcome - we have a comfy corner to rest, offer water bowls and snacks to buy.
